About OsloMET Gaming

OsloMet Gaming is a student-led community at OsloMet that aims to bring students together through shared interests in video gaming and esports. This group provides a welcoming, inclusive environment where students from various academic backgrounds can participate in game nights, social gatherings, and esports events. By fostering connections and teamwork, OsloMet Gaming supports both casual and competitive gaming experiences, encouraging students to enjoy and develop skills in a collaborative setting.

Aligned with OsloMet's focus on technology and innovation, OsloMet Gaming integrates gaming into a larger educational framework. The university supports projects that explore the role of gaming as a space for social inclusion and skill-building. For example, one of its research initiatives examines how gaming can be a platform for youth to connect, gain digital skills, and enhance social interactions. This emphasis on digital culture and technology underscores OsloMet's commitment to creating professional and social opportunities through modern digital tools and platforms.

How to join?

OsloMet Gaming is open to everyone, regardless of prior gaming experience. You can join whether you are a student at SiO (Studentsamskipnaden i Oslo) or not. To apply for membership, you need to contact the organization. You can do this by sending an email or a direct message via Facebook or Discord. In your message, include your first and last name, your student status (whether you are a SiO student), and the duration of your membership (one or two semesters). Optionally, you may also provide your email address, date of birth, and Discord username.

There is a membership fee of 200 NOK per semester. This fee comes with several benefits, including free entry to all events during your membership period, access to the office (when a board member is present), and voting rights at general assemblies. If you choose not to become a member, you can still participate in many activities. Non-members can access the Discord server, enjoy free entry to most online events, attend open lounges, and participate in physical events for a small fee.
